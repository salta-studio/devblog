[waves] a meta-anthology

April 13, 2025 by he and her

A meta-anthology, as I define it, is just like any other anthology. There is a central theme. The addition is that the character, or characters, of each story is the same abstraction. The character is living experiences through different timelines or reincarnations.

I recently messed up my life again. This time at least I forgave myself right away.

In the process, I came up with Maga. I thought that perhaps I can write about things I have learnt from others, especially the women in my life.

Maga then becomes this abstraction or crystal. I came up with 8 different stories or manifestations of Maga. 

I plan to finish this meta-anthology today, however, I have vacations coming which I mostly will spend them on painting my house but I figure perhaps I can write the pending stories in breaks.

Here's the current status.

A wave is a disturbance or oscillation that transfers energy from one point to another. If we are talking about the building blocks of reality, these pieces of energy can behave like particles or waves. 
    But the waves or echoes you are about to read, dear reader, cannot be rationalized. There is no math to explain them. The only way to understand them is by experiencing them.
