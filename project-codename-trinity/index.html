It all comes down to this: project codename TRINITY.

Let's back up a bit.

I majored in theoretical physics so the following will come a bit as a red flag: I had a dread but I don't see as such but rather as the future that I will meet eventually. I have this weird believe that dreams are not necessarily simple projects of our subconscious. If telepathy is possible then we can affect our dreams too. And I do believe such thing is possible because I have learnt that consciousness is eternal.
I know, right? What the heck am I saying? All possible credibility you may have given me now is gone. Honestly, I don't blame you.

There is no other way to say that, it is what is. If you just suspense your disbelief in the name of art, maybe you will understand it.

Anyway, I had a vision of my future. I saw the books. Atlas' Saga. That's funny, though. I never intended to have more than one book. Atlas ended up been 6 books. I don't recall the exact number I dreamt but I knew in the dream that it was my children books. Once I had that dream I said: I cannot longer postpone this. Gotta do it and gotta do it now!

So I embarked on the journey. I started looking for an artist to illustrated the story. Back then I haven't finished the first book and only book. Once I met the illustrator (koimoons), a lot of strange things happened. It was almost like I became this shaman. I started to dream again. I don't usually dream (perhaps that's why I give dreams so much weight). For the purpose of protecting the future, I won't say what those dreams I have provided me. All I can say is a lot and they are influencing all my work.

While I was talking to koimoons, I started to get all these thoughts and visions, if you will. Maybe I'm crazy and I daydream a lot. It doesn't matter. I started to see more on Atlas' journey. I had one book and suddenly it was 6 books.Â 

I finished the first book even before the time I was expecting and then 2 month later I finished all 6 books. I was, in other words, on fire!

While talking to koimoonsÂ and knowing more about her and her artistic creation I came to the conclusion that Viatorem, koimoons' character,Â it had to be involve in Atlas' Saga. Two reason, one, I feel so in debt with koimoons 'cuz she has managed to create the perfect visualization of Atlas. I cannot conceive how she managed that. I have this theory that humans are like antennas so perhaps it was meant to be (honestly, without much detail, it was... dreams but that's story I cannot tell... yet and not here).

As a gift to koimoons and for other reasons too I decided that Viatorem will be my long-run project for decades to come. I had this story called The Last Inuit (which is my nickname thelastinuit). It was about a bunch of scientists, artists and philosophers that escape Earth and travel the cosmos having space adventures. I never finished that story and Viatorem revived that dream hence the reason to take over koimoons' character.

Now, what the heck all this has to do with trinity, anyway? Well, a lot. You see, there is this meta-dimension called Crystal Children. It's the only thing that's actually real. The rest of things are just meta-projections of all those abstractions. There is one and only one abstraction. To avoid spoilers, if any, I will not mention that abstraction is. Nonetheless, I can say that that abstractions became 3, in other words, reality is a relation.

Those 3 abstractions play games and dance around. They are all around but most of the time we don't see them because, well, we are abstractions too. the way it works is a bit chaotic. I'll say this: everything is nothing and vice versa. When you understand that or rather just accept/believe that you can see the reality that is hidden from us.

All I'm doing therefore is being the instrument that allows those abstractions to manifest (stories) and koimoons is the one who illustrate them.
