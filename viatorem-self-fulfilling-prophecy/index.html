[viatorem] self-fulfilling prophecy

March 19, 2025 by he and her

Ain't consider myself an expert on anything. I'm more like an observant, a very curious person. I'm not interested in being judgemental but rather know more about anything. When it comes to writing or any creative process, my way to go is to be surrounded by things that project you into those scenarios.

I just realized that I have become, in a way, Viatorem. I think that's why I fell in love with that character. I'm in my house at 6am working with Lola, a tiny bunny. I recently pierced my ears again and the design I chose is like a representation of hey eyes. I started to see my home as a spaceship. In the afternoons, I take a break and start dancing in the open space I have. Viatorem's adventure will always start with a song and her dancing in her spaceship. I'm living in a desert city and I love the desert.

I have in front of me, for lack of a better word, a self-fulfilling prophecy. I mean, I intentionally but also subconsciously did it.

What I find very interesting is that Viatorem was not my creation. She was created by koimoons. Now I find myself not being able to live without her. Don't get me wrong. I love Atlas' story. I created her. She's in my room as we speak but Viatorem seems to fulfill a very old dream: to travel the Cosmos and have space adventures, my own personal space opera.

I'm grateful for what my life has become especially with this forsaken global situation.
