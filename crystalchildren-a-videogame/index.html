[crystal.children] a videogame?

March 17, 2025 by The Alkemyth

I've been thinking that perhaps Crystal Children is not a video game.

Yes, you play the character, Atlas. However, I think what I want from the player is to experience solitude but also adventure while playing in the desert.

I want the player to feel awe while sliding through the dunes at night.

I want the player to feel lost and at the same time to have hope for the future.

I want the player to be in the world and feel part of it, part of the world and not separate from it.

Is that a video game? I have no idea. I have played a few games that didn't feel like a game but rather a cinematographic experience.
